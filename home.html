    Hemp Wacko Strains

    The Cannabis family contains hemp and marijuana species. Hemp and Marijuna differ in the amount of THC (tetrahydrocannabinol), the psychoactive stuff, content in the dried plant matter. Hemp will contain a max level of 0.3% THC, that's less than 1 percent. Premium marijuna crops can have up to 30% THC. Wacko strains are all tested to have a max level of THC under the 0.3% limit.
